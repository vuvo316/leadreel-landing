Frequently asked questions

Which lead sources do you support?

Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, Homes.com, and any lead form that sends an email notification. If it hits your inbox, LeadReel can respond to it.

Is this TCPA compliant?

Yes. LeadReel only sends SMS to leads who have explicitly submitted their phone number through a real estate lead form. This is considered an inbound inquiry response, not cold messaging.

What if I want to customise the response?

You write the template. Every response uses your voice, your name, your phone number. You can write different templates for different lead sources.

Can I try it before paying?

Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. If we fail to respond within 60 seconds during your trial, the entire trial month is free.

How is this different from Follow Up Boss or BoomTown?

Those are enterprise CRMs for teams. They cost $200-300+/mo and take weeks to set up. LeadReel is a single-purpose tool for individual agents: connect, write your template, and you're done in 5 minutes. No training required.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep access until the end of your billing period. No data is held hostage. Cancel with one click from the dashboard.
